
# AI-Powered Parametric Insurance Platform for Gig Delivery Workers

## 1. Problem Statement

India’s gig economy delivery workers (such as food, grocery, and e-commerce delivery partners) face frequent income loss due to external disruptions like extreme weather, pollution, and sudden zone closures. These workers depend on daily earnings, and when such disruptions occur, they lose a significant portion of their income.

Currently, there is no simple insurance system that protects gig workers from these uncontrollable income disruptions.

Our solution proposes an **AI-powered parametric insurance platform** that automatically protects delivery workers against income loss caused by environmental and social disruptions.

---

# 2. Target Persona

### Selected Delivery Persona

Food Delivery Workers (Example: Swiggy / Zomato delivery partners)

### Characteristics

* Works 6–8 hours per day
* Earns income per delivery order
* Income depends on working hours and number of orders
* Highly affected by weather conditions and local disruptions

### Location Focus

Urban Indian cities where delivery demand is high.

---

# 3. Key Disruptions Covered

Our insurance platform focuses only on **income loss caused by external disruptions**, not health or vehicle damages.

### Environmental Disruptions

* Heavy rainfall
* Flooded roads
* Extreme heat
* Severe air pollution

### Social Disruptions

* Sudden curfews
* Local strikes
* Zone closures

### Example Parametric Triggers

* Rainfall > 80mm in a day
* Temperature > 45°C
* Air Quality Index > 400
* Official curfew announcement

When these conditions occur, the system automatically detects the event and triggers payouts.

---

# 4. Weekly Premium Model

Gig workers earn on a weekly cycle, so our insurance uses a **weekly pricing model**.

### Example Pricing

| Weekly Premium | Income Protection |
| -------------- | ----------------- |
| ₹10/week       | ₹500 coverage     |
| ₹20/week       | ₹1000 coverage    |

Premiums are dynamically calculated based on:

* Location risk
* Historical weather patterns
* Frequency of disruptions
* Worker activity data

---

# 5. System Workflow

1. Worker Registration
2. Location and profile verification
3. AI-based risk assessment
4. Weekly premium calculation
5. Worker purchases insurance policy
6. System monitors real-time disruption data using APIs
7. Disruption detected (rain, pollution, curfew, etc.)
8. Claim automatically triggered
9. Instant payout to worker

This ensures **fast and automated compensation without manual claim processes**.

---

# 6. AI / ML Integration

Artificial Intelligence will be used in multiple parts of the system.

### Dynamic Risk Assessment

AI models will analyze:

* Historical weather data
* Geographic risk zones
* Delivery activity data

### Premium Optimization

Machine learning will adjust premiums depending on the risk level of the worker's operating area.

### Fraud Detection

AI will detect:

* Duplicate claims
* Fake weather claims
* GPS spoofing
* Unusual claim patterns

# 8. Parametric Automation System

The platform continuously monitors external disruption data through APIs.

**Example Flow**

Weather API detects heavy rainfall  
            ↓  
System validates disruption threshold  
            ↓  
Checks workers in affected zone  
            ↓  
Insurance policy verified  
            ↓  
Automatic claim initiated  
            ↓  
Instant payout processed  

This removes the need for manual claim filing.
